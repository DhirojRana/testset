SAMOSA

Description

Samosa is a deep-fried snack with a crisp pastry shell and spiced potato-pea filling. First, make a firm dough with flour, salt, oil, and water. Boil and mash potatoes, then mix with peas, cumin, chili, and salt for the filling. Roll the dough, cut into halves, shape into cones, and stuff with the potato mixture. Seal the edges and deep-fry until golden brown. Best served with mint or tamarind chutney.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • boiled potatoes
  • peas
  • cumin
  • chili
  • salt
  • oil

Steps

  1. Make dough with flour, salt, oil, and water.
  2. Mash boiled potatoes with peas and spices.
  3. Roll dough, cut into halves, and shape into cones.
  4. Fill with potato mix and seal.
  5. Deep-fry until golden and crispy.
