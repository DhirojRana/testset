MOMO

Description

Momo is a popular Nepali dumpling filled with spiced minced meat or vegetables. To make it, knead flour and water into a soft dough, and let it rest. Prepare the filling by mixing ground meat (like chicken or buff) or finely chopped veggies with onion, garlic, ginger, green chili, and salt. Roll out the dough into small circles, add the filling, and fold them into dumpling shapes. Steam them for 10–15 minutes and serve hot with spicy tomato chutney.

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • minced meat/veg
  • onion
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • chili
  • salt
  • oil

Steps:

  1. Knead flour with water to make soft dough.
  2. Mix meat/veg with spices, onion, garlic, and salt.
  3. Roll dough into small circles.
  4. Fill with mixture, fold and seal.
  5. Steam 10–15 mins and serve with achar.
