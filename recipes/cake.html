CAKE

Description

This is a simple vanilla sponge cake perfect for beginners. Cream together butter and sugar until fluffy, then mix in eggs and vanilla essence. Sift flour and baking powder together and fold into the wet ingredients along with milk to make a smooth batter. Pour it into a greased baking pan and bake at 180°C (350°F) for 30–35 minutes. Let it cool, then enjoy plain or with frosting.
