NOODLES

Description

Stir-fried noodles are a quick and tasty meal made with boiled noodles and veggies. Start by boiling noodles and setting them aside. In a pan, sauté garlic in oil, then add sliced vegetables like cabbage, carrot, and onion. Stir-fry for a couple of minutes, then add soy sauce, chili sauce, and a pinch of salt. Add the noodles back in, toss everything together well, and serve hot.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • mixed vegetables
  • garlic
  • soy sauce
  • chili
  • oil

Steps

  1. Boil noodles, drain, and set aside.
  2. Sauté garlic in oil.
  3. Add veggies and stir-fry 2 mins.
  4. Mix in soy sauce and chili.
  5. Add noodles, toss well, and serve hot.
